Top Spots on Fidalgo Island

Fidalgo Island is a gorgeous little island that is part of the San Juan Islands in the northern region of the Puget Sound. Situated on Fidalgo Island is the town of Anacortes, Washington. There are tons of great things to do outside on Fidalgo Island. To help you plan a great trip, we've provided a list of spots that are worth a visit.
Spot Description Difficulty
Washington Park Large outdoor park on the waterfront. Includes a sceninc 2 mile paved trail and countless side trails. Also includes boat launch, fire pits, play grounds, and camp sites. Easy
Cap Sante Overlook Rocky cliff area overlooking Cap Sante Marina and downtown Anacortes as well as the refineries. Can be simply viewed from stepping outside of car, or can be explored via hiking. Easy/Moderate
Whistle Lake Cliff Jumping A series of cliffs from 5 feet to 60 feet that can be jumped off of into Whistle Lake. There's a hike to get out to the cliff locations, and you must be prepared to swim to some of the jumping locations as well as back to shore. Moderate
Mt. Erie The tallest point on Fidalgo Island. You can either walk/hike to the top, or drive your car. The adventurous people climb the side of the mountain. There are several trails you can explore from the top and incredible views of Fidalgo Island, Whidbey Island, and the San Juans, as well as the mainland. Heavy
