Did you know that you can have a pig as a housepet? This is a very exciting thing that many people don't realize. How cool is it that you can live with a pig!

Why live with a pig?

Now, you may be asking yourself why you'd want to live with a pig when you can live with a more conventional pet like a dog or a cat. Here are a few reasons why a pig is a great choice:
  • Contrary to popular belief, they are very clean.
  • They are quick learners. You can potty train a pig in as little as one day!
  • They love belly rubs, and you love to give belly rubs. Right?
  • They can be walked on a leash, which is obviously even more adorable than walking a puppy on a leash.
